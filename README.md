## 🚀 Overview

**Outseek** is a browser-based simulated email environment designed to recreate the look and feel of a real workplace inbox (similar to Outlook).

It’s built to help individuals:
- Transition back into a work mindset  
- Reduce anxiety around returning to employment  
- Rebuild familiarity with day-to-day digital work environments  

---

## 🧠 Why Outseek Exists

Returning to work after time away can feel overwhelming.  
Outseek provides a **safe, pressure-free environment** that mimics a professional setting, allowing users to ease themselves back into routine.

This is especially useful for:
- Job seekers  
- Career returners  
- Individuals managing anxiety or burnout  
- People who benefit from structure and familiarity  

---

## ✨ Features

- 📥 **Realistic Inbox UI**  
  A clean, familiar interface inspired by modern workplace email systems

- 📨 **Simulated Work Emails**  
  Example messages like meeting invites, reports, and internal comms

- 🧩 **Familiar Navigation**  
  Inbox, Sent Items, Drafts, Junk, and more

- 🧠 **Mental Reconditioning** *(Planned)*  
  Gentle prompts and support to help users rebuild work habits

- 🎯 **Low Pressure Environment**  
  No real consequences — just practice and familiarity

---

## 🛠️ Use Cases

- Practising daily work routines  
- Reducing "first day back" anxiety  
- Demonstrating UI/UX concepts  
- Personal productivity simulation
